About Jamia Hamdard

Jamia Hamdard is a renowned university located in New Delhi, India. It was established in 1989 by Hakeem Abdul Hameed as a deemed-to-be-university under Section 3 of the University Grants Commission (UGC) Act 1956. The university is named after Hakeem Abdul Hameed's great-grandfather, Hakim Abdul Hamid, a renowned practitioner of Unani medicine.

Here are some key points about Jamia Hamdard:

  • Focus on Unani Medicine: One of the distinctive features of Jamia Hamdard is its emphasis on traditional systems of medicine, particularly Unani medicine.
  • Multi-disciplinary Approach: While its roots lie in Unani medicine, Jamia Hamdard offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines including pharmacy, nursing, management, science, humanities, and law.
  • Research and Innovation: The university is actively involved in research and innovation across different fields.
  • Recognition and Accreditation: Jamia Hamdard is recognized by various regulatory bodies including the University Grants Commission (UGC), the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), and the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI).
  • Campus and Facilities: The university campus is spread over a sprawling area in South Delhi and provides modern infrastructure and facilities for academic, research, and extracurricular activities.
  • Global Outreach: Jamia Hamdard has established collaborations with universities and research institutions worldwide.
  • Social Responsibility: The university is committed to social responsibility and community engagement.

Academic Programs

Jamia Hamdard offers a comprehensive range of academic programs at undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels across various disciplines:

  • Pharmacy: Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm), Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharm), Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D)
  • Medicine: Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS), Doctor of Medicine (MD), Master of Surgery (MS)
  • Nursing: Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.Sc Nursing), Master of Science in Nursing (M.Sc Nursing)
  • Management: Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Computer Science: Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), Master of Computer Applications (MCA)
  • Law: Bachelor of Laws (LLB), Master of Laws (LLM)
  • Science: Bachelor of Science (B.Sc), Master of Science (M.Sc) in various disciplines such as Biochemistry, Biotechnology, Microbiology, etc.
  • Humanities: Bachelor of Arts (BA), Master of Arts (MA) in subjects like English, History, Economics, etc.
  • Unani Medicine: Bachelor of Unani Medicine and Surgery (BUMS), Doctor of Medicine in Unani (MD Unani), Master of Surgery in Unani (MS Unani)

Facilities

Jamia Hamdard is equipped with world-class facilities to support the academic and extracurricular needs of its students:

  • Libraries: The university boasts well-stocked libraries with a vast collection of books, journals, and online resources.
  • Laboratories: State-of-the-art laboratories are available for practical learning and research in various disciplines including pharmacy, medicine, science, and technology.
  • Sports Complex: The campus features modern sports facilities including indoor and outdoor sports arenas for students to engage in physical activities and sports competitions.
  • Hostel Accommodation: Residential facilities are provided for both male and female students with comfortable hostel accommodations and amenities.
  • Medical Facilities: The university campus includes medical facilities such as a hospital, clinics, and medical services to cater to the healthcare needs of students and staff.
  • Research Centers: Various research centers and institutes are established within the campus to promote research and innovation in different fields.
  • Computer Labs: Well-equipped computer labs with high-speed internet connectivity are available for students to enhance their technical skills and access online resources.
  • Conference Halls and Auditoriums: Spacious conference halls and auditoriums are available for organizing seminars, conferences, and cultural events.

Glimpses of the University

Centre for Media and Mass Communication Studies

Jamia Hamdard launched the School of Media Education and Mass communication which was later renamed as Centre for Media and Mass Communication Studies, to provide academic and professional excellence in this field. The Centre has started with a three years degree in Bachelor of Arts in Journalism and Mass Communication with a capacity of 50 students in the year 2020. The innovative course curriculum has been designed to equip the students to meet the challenges of dynamically evolving and highly competitive discipline. The Centre aims to provide the students with world-class teaching and training to churn out skilled manpower and informed scholars to serve National and International Communities and Media Industries.

B.A. in Journalism and Mass Communication

The B.A. in Journalism and Mass Communication course has been designed to train aspiring media candidates in various industry and technical skills of audio-video editing, anchoring, content writing, photography, media reporting, etc. These professionals would be employed with TV news channels, Radio Broadcasting, Print Media, Digital Journalism, and in the Government Communication set-up. This course also enhances entrepreneurial skills and training to set up their own production units.
